## About Tokens of Thought

Welcome to **Tokens of Thought** — a blog dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of Artificial Intelligence, one concept at a time.

Here, we dive deep into the mathematics, architectures, and engineering behind modern AI systems. From the inner workings of Transformer attention mechanisms to the design of agentic systems and inference optimization techniques, each post aims to build genuine understanding — not just surface-level familiarity.

Whether you're a researcher, engineer, or curious learner, you'll find detailed explorations with mathematical rigor, clear explanations, and practical insights.

### Topics Covered

- **Transformers & Attention** — The architecture powering modern AI
- **Agentic Systems** — Designing AI that can reason, plan, and act
- **Inference Optimization** — Making models faster and more efficient
- **Training Techniques** — How models learn from data
- **Emerging Research** — What's next in AI
